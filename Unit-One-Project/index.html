Welcome to Task Buckets!!

Welcome to the task tracking game Task Buckets! The purpose of this game is designed to help you keep track of your tasks in a fun and interactive way. With this game, you'll be able to easily manage your tasks and increase your productivity.

How to Play

The game is very simple to play. To get started, lets show you around:

Tasks Button

The Task button is located at the center of the screen directly below the backboard. When pressed, the Task button will have a few functionalities. You will be able to see what tasks you have to do for the day and what number they are assigned to. Remember the number!! You will need it to shoot the ball into the basket. You can also enter a task and press the add button and the corresponding task and number will show up. You can also remove your tasks by pressing rseet!

Submit Button

Once you have accomplished the task, enter the number in the prompt box! Press the shoot! button and your task will be tracked! Please remember to enter a valid task number!

Stats Button

Once you've completed a task, click on the "Stat" button next to the task in the list. This will mark down the task number accomplished and you can refer back to the Task button see what tasks you did! Furthermore, when the next day button is pressed, all the tasks accomplished will be cleared!

Next Day Button

Once you are complete for the day, or the day is over, whichever comes first, you press the next day button. You are your biggest oppponent. So when the next day button is pressed, not only is all the information cleared from the stat tab, but the home score updates to 0 and the opponents score updates to the score from the day before. The purpose of each day is to try to defeat yourself from the day before!

Reset the Game

If you want to start over, click on the "next day" button twice. The first time will move the score from the home to the opponents side, and the second time will clear the scoreboard to 0!

Conclusion

That's it! With this game, you'll be able to keep track of your tasks and increase your productivity. We hope you enjoy playing Task Buckets!!!!!
